先生からの推薦書頂きました！

July 06, 2022 3510

As Zhang Huanming’s undergraduate advisor, I have instructed him to complete his university studies and academic graduation thesis.

Zhang huanming always has strong ambitions and works hard for them. Once he set a goal to strive for, he will be persistent until he succeeds. There was a period in his freshman year when he felt overwhelmed. However, he quickly found where to lay his passion after communicating with me, and began to focus on training his programming ability and explore his love for video and media. Soon after, Zhang Huanming chose to study Digital Media Technology, and got excellent grades in professional courses, such as Introductory Digital Media Technology. After deciding on pursuing further education in Japan, he also obtained the foreign language proficiency certifications in both Japanese and English within one year.

Zhang Huanming also has the courage to keep on trying without fear of failure. In the second-year English Listening & Speaking courses, he did not shy away from delivering presentations in public despite the nervousness. Instead, he practiced hard and eventually felt ease with presentation. He insisted that he would rather be wrong than do nothing, and that success happens after failed attempts. It was this spirit of learning that got him a score of 91 out of 100 in the Workplace English course in his junior year.

The most outstanding quality that Zhang Huanming possesses is that he is keen on improving and applying the skills learned into real projects. For example, in the third year of university, students were asked to complete the development of a 3D game using the Unity software for an assignment from the Multi-media Interactive Computer Design course. There were only two members in Zhang Huanming’s group, so he had to learn modeling and animation effects besides finishing the coding by himself. Additionally, he is also interested in the UI design of software, and he often goes online to study design theories. And he has successfully developed multiple applications catering to various needs, during which process he combined programming technology and did everything himself from interface design all the way to background logic development. It has laid a solid foundation for the off-campus internship in the senior year and the employment after graduation.

The research direction of Zhang Huanming’s graduation thesis is “Application of Internet Technology in Medical Field”. Under my instruction, he has had remarkable performance on this topic through his independent research of relevant materials, analysis of competing products on the market, step-by-step revision and refinement of his ideas. At the same time, he often participated in various APP production projects during his school days. Aiming to develop excellent practical applications, his accomplishments have covered education, entertainment, finance and other aspects, which can be seen from his research results. Moreover, Zhang Huanming stays passionate about the visual representation of data, as how to present it intuitively to customers is what he wants to achieve.

In view of the above, I would like to recommend Zhang Huanming to your laboratory. With the opportunity to further study and develop in the good academic environment and research atmosphere of your university, I am convinced that he will be a valuable asset not only to his field of study, but also to academics and society.

I sincerely encourage you to look favorably upon his application.
